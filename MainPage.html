MAGIC CAKE

Magic cake is one of the tastiest cakes in the world.These are the making stories.

You can view our legal page for more information.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter
  • 3 cups of flour

Directions

  1. Mix eggs butter flour and sugar in a large bowl
  2. Spread into a non stick dish
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for one hour
  4. Let it sit at room temperature for 20 min
  5. Eat and enjoy!!!
